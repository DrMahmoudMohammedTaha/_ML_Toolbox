1. Programming Languages - Master Python and R to kickstart your AI journey.
2. Mathematics & Statistics - Build a strong foundation in Linear Algebra, Probability & Statistics, and Calculus.
3. Data Handling - Learn Data Preprocessing, Cleaning, and Visualization techniques to work efficiently with data.
4. Machine Learning - Dive into Supervised and Unsupervised Learning, and explore Reinforcement Learning.
5. Deep Learning - Understand the concepts of Neural Networks, CNNs, RNNs, and GANs.
6. NLP (Natural Language Processing) - Gain expertise in Text Preprocessing, Sentiment Analysis, and Language Models like BERT and GPT.
7. Computer Vision - Master Image Processing, Object Detection, and Image Segmentation.
8. Big Data Technologies - Familiarize yourself with Hadoop and Spark to handle large datasets.
9. AI Frameworks & Libraries - Get hands-on experience with TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Scikit-Learn.
10. Model Deployment - Learn to deploy models using Flask/Django, Docker for Containerization, and Kubernetes for Orchestration.
11. Cloud Platforms Explore AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud for scalable AI solutions.
12. AI Ethics - Understand the importance of Bias and Fairness, and Privacy in AI.
13. Version Control Systems Use Git for effective collaboration and version control.
14. Experiment Tracking Track your experiments with MLflow and Weights & Biases.
15. MLOps - Implement CI/CD for ML, and ensure robust Model Monitoring and Maintenance.
16. AI in Production - Learn about Real-Time Inference and Scaling AI Models for production environments.
